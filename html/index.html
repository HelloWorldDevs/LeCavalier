MEET THE TEAM

Ross M. LeCavalier, DDS

A native of the Chicago area, Dr. LeVacalier graduated from the University of Illinois College of Dentistry in 1986. Prior to studying at Dental School, he attended the University of Illinois. Dr. LeCavalier has been practicing for over 20 years and is recognized for being one of the top dental offices in Denver, Colorado.

Since he began practicing dentistry, Dr. LeVacalier has kept up to date on many continuing educational classes, ensuring that his patients receive the most comprehensive and advanced dental care.

Dr. LeCavalier completed post-graduate training at the prestigious Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies under the world-renowned Dr. William Dickerson. While at the Institute, Dr. LeCavalier underwent intensive training including completing complex cases on live patients.

Dr. LeCavalier is one of the few dentists from Colorado that has completed a program that teaches these skills.

Dr. LeCavalier's commitment to excellence is emphasized bu undergoing this training to provide patients with only the best restorations and care possible.



Stephanie

Stephanie is originally a native of England and graduated from the University of Colorado with a B.A. in Biology. She has been in the field of dentistry for 12 years, beginning as a dental assistant. Her extensive and varied knowledge of dentistry can help her answer all of your questions.

Stephanie is proud to be part of such an excellent team of cosmetic and family dentists here in our dental office in Denver where we are dedicated to the highest standard of patient care, in a comfortable and caring atmosphere.

Stephanie is married with two young boys. She and her family enjoy cmaping, biking, sports and travel.
